<objective>
Add `scope_for/1` helpers to `Foglet.Threads` and `Foglet.Posts` per D-08. These single-line delegating functions synthesize the `{:board, board_id}` scope tuple from a domain struct so that future callers (Phase 8 moderation operations) never have to hardcode `{:board, thread.board_id}` inline.

Purpose: D-08 mandates per-domain `scope_for/1` helpers for consistent scope synthesis at call sites. Action atoms for thread and post operator functions already exist in the `@valid_actions` allowlist from Plan 01, but their authorization call sites are deferred to Phase 8 (D-20). This plan delivers the last piece of the Phase 1 seam — the scope helpers themselves — so Phase 8 is a mechanical "add the `Bodyguard.permit/4` line" diff with no missing helpers.

This plan does NOT signature-change `lock_thread`, `unlock_thread`, `sticky_thread`, `unsticky_thread`, `move_thread`, `delete_thread`, `delete_post`, or `edit_post`. D-20 is explicit: those are Phase 8 work.

Output: Two single-line functions plus tiny unit tests, bringing the MODR-02 data-visibility seam to completion for Phase 1.
</objective>
