## Overview

v1.0.2 was a restraint workstream — a retrospective audit of all 9 TUI screens shipped through `phase-03-polish`. The dominant risk was diligent over-execution (Milestones 4/5/6/9 all claim the whitespace that existed), so every phase enforced a hard `AUDIT-16` rule: equal or lower line count AND equal or lower visible row count. No new functionality was added. Two cross-cutting helpers were extracted (Phase 0), then applied phase-by-phase across all 9 screens.

- `Input.TextInput` adoption in Login locked by user with accepted `█`-cursor visual drift — inherited by Register and NewThread
