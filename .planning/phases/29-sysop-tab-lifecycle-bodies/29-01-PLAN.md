<objective>
Establish the Phase 29 lifecycle foundation: convert the four Sysop submodule slots
(`:boards_view`, `:limits_form`, `:system_snapshot`, `:users_view`) from
`term() | nil` to a tagged `:not_loaded | :loading | {:loaded, struct} | {:error, reason}`
enum, wire the four App-level load triads (modeled verbatim on the Moderation
load triad at `app.ex:513-533, 1081-1116`), trigger dispatch from both the
tab-changed branch in `Sysop.handle_key/2` (D-05) and a first-entry guard in
`Sysop.init/1` (D-06), and rewrite `render_tab_body/3` + `delegate_to_submodule/5`
to honor the tagged enum. Remove all five `"Press any key to load"` placeholders
and forbid direct `Raxol.Core.Runtime.Command.task` calls from any
`Foglet.TUI.Screens.Sysop.*` module (D-04).

Purpose: This is the substrate every other Phase 29 plan depends on. Without
the tagged enum + App-level loads, no error/retry/forbidden path exists, no
USERS keybind gating can run on a focused row's status, and Sysop tabs remain
gated behind the "Press any key" anti-pattern.

Output: Modified `state.ex`, `app.ex`, `sysop.ex`; extended `sysop_test.exs`
with a `describe "lifecycle"` block covering tagged-enum round-trip,
tab-switch auto-load, first-entry dispatch, idempotent re-entry, and
`Press any key`/`Raxol.Core.Runtime.Command.task` grep guards.
</objective>
